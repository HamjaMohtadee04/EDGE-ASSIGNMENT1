Course We Offer

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Chittagong University offers a range of academic programs, including BSc, MSc, and MPhil. These programs are designed to provide students with in-depth knowledge and skills in computer science, preparing them for successful careers in academia, research, and the tech industry.

BSC

The undergraduate program covers foundational and advanced courses such as Introduction to Programming, Data Structures and Algorithms, Database Systems, Computer Networks, Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, and Machine Learning.

MSC

The master's program focuses on advanced topics and research, offering courses like Advanced Algorithms, Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and Deep Learning.

M.Phil

The MPhil program is research-intensive, allowing students to delve deeper into areas such as Ontology Development, Advanced Machine Learning Techniques, High-Performance Computing, and Cyber Attack Detection Frameworks.

Our Facilities

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Chittagong University offers a range of modern facilities to support learning and research. These include fully equipped computer labs, advanced research centers, a well-stocked library, and high-speed internet access. Our facilities are designed to create an environment where students can thrive academically and innovate effectively.

24/7 LAB

High Quality Classroom
